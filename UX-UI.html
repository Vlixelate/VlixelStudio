Wireframe, UX, UI, and Web Creation

This group project focused on creating an app to track bus arrival schedules. As the team lead, I guided our research with key questions:

  • Why was this application created?
  • Who is our target user?
  • What problems do users face with transportation?
  • How can the app help during rush hours?
  • When do users typically open the app?

These questions helped us design a user-friendly and intuitive information architecture. Although we faced challenges with tight deadline, our teamwork and communication led to a successful project.

Our research ensured the app is easy to use day or night, simple to navigate, and compatible with all mobile devices.
